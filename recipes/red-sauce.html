Red Sauce

Description

This is a dope recipe for red sauce that my wife and I use all the time. It's good for pasta, meatball subs, or even on eggs! It's one of those recipes you pull out to impress your friends.

Now, I'll level with you: this recipe was stolen from Joshua Weismann's 2 Million Dollar special video on YouTube. He uses it in a chicken parm that is truly amazing. However, most of us don't have the time, energy, or inclination to deepfry on a regular basis. This sauce goes beyone that, I promise.

Ingredients

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of thyme
  • 2 serano peppers
  • 4 slices of thick-cut bacon
  • 25g of garlic
  • 1 tsp of sugar
  • 1 TBSP of salt
  • 1/4 cup of parmesean cheese
  • Red Pepper Flakes to taste

Steps

  • Cut up your stuff--real small like.
  • Cook the bacon in a sauce pan until it starts to get crispy
  • Add garlic and pepper
  • Cook until it's all super crispy
  • Take it out of the pan and cut it up even smaller. Tiny, tiny bits. Then put it back in the pan.
  • Add the tomatoes and the other stuff
  • Cook it for about 15 minutes
  • That's it dude. You're done. What, do you want a medal?
